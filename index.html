Coming March 1st, 2021.

Sage helps you create & educate without constraint.

Teaching online is not easily. Managing live lessons, scheduling multiple classes, keeping track of attendance, handing out assignments, sharing content and communicating with students is an extremely complicated process.

Sage does the heavy lifting of managing your lessons from start to finish. We want to enable you to focus on your craft and nurture learners to learn in the best social environment.

All in one lesson hosting.

Go live with all your students with a click of a button. Schedule calendar events to hold personalized and engaging discussions with your students. We'll manage the class settings for you.

Manage and share content easily.

Share and manage all your resources with students securely in one single place. You no longer have to worry about files on multiple chats, emails or 3rd party file hosting platforms.

Understand and build your audience.

Track student analytics to follow your students' progress. Facilitate conversations in the forum to reach out to more students and build your education community.

Sage empowers every educator to focus on their craft and build the future of education.
